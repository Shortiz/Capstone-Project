Community Outreach

American Bank is more than a financial institution in the communites we serve; we are a part of them as well. We help to make our communities better place to live and work by being an important contributor of time, talent, and resources.

Community First, the Bank's employee charitable and social committee, is in its 38th year of fundraising. Since its inception in 1983, the Committee has raised every donation internally, reflecting Peoples Bank employees' commitment to dig deep and make a dedicated effort to improve the quality of life in the communities in which we work and live.

In 2020, through generous employee donations and matching funds from the Bank, Community First distributed more than $70,000 to benefit organizations such as the Boys and Girls Clubs of Northwest PA, Haven Palace, Legacy Foundation, Food Bank of Northwest PA, Cancer Resource Center, and Opportunity Enterprises.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, the Committee donated $28,651 to local Northwest PA nonprofit organizations over a six-month period through their Casual for a Cause initiative. The initiative was put into motion as a way to help local communities be more successful at a time when they needed it most.
